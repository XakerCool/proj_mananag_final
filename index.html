The Night Library

The Night Library is an innovative project designed to address the unmet need for round-the-clock study and resource access in Astana. Recognizing the increasing demand for public spaces that support nighttime study, work, and research, the Night Library aims to create a network of 24-hour libraries. Our vision is to provide residents with access to both physical and digital resources, as well as technology, in a comfortable, quiet environment that fosters learning and personal growth.
